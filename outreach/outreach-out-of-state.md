## Outreach Strategy for Out-of-State

1. **Lead with the free pilot** — same pitch, but emphasize "no geographic barrier, works on any device"
2. **Community colleges first** — they're more responsive than universities and have direct workforce development goals
3. **HBCUs and minority-serving institutions** — actively looking for career readiness tools, often underserved
4. **State college systems** — one yes can unlock multiple campuses
5. **LinkedIn warm intros** — connect with career center directors before emailing
6. **Mention Product Hunt launch** — once live, social proof helps flip "maybe" into "yes"
