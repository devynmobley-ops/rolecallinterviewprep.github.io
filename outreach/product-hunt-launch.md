# RoleCall — Product Hunt Launch Kit

## Tagline
Practice mock interviews for 374+ careers — free

## Description
RoleCall helps job seekers prepare for interviews across every industry — healthcare, tech, trades, business, education, creative, service, legal, and sales.

Pick your career, answer 12 role-specific interview questions, and get expert tips on what interviewers actually want to hear. Track your progress, compare your scores, and build confidence before the real thing.

**What makes RoleCall different:**
- 374+ roles with 12 curated questions each — not generic lists
- Expert tips for every question explaining what hiring managers look for
- Education & certification paths so you know what credentials to pursue
- Progress tracking with scores, streaks, and session history
- Works on any device — no download required
- Core experience is completely free

**For schools & universities:** Bulk promo codes, student progress tracking, and institutional packages. Students can practice on their own time, and career centers can see engagement.

Built by two people who believe everyone deserves access to quality interview prep — not just those who can afford a coach.

Hey Product Hunt! 👋

We built RoleCall because interview prep shouldn't cost hundreds of dollars or require a personal coach.

The idea came from watching friends and family struggle with interviews — not because they weren't qualified, but because they didn't know what questions to expect or what interviewers were really looking for.

So we built a free tool that covers 374+ careers with role-specific questions and expert tips. Whether you're a nurse, software engineer, electrician, teacher, or sales rep — RoleCall has questions tailored to your field.

A few things we're proud of:
- Every role has exactly 12 questions — curated, not auto-generated
- Expert tips explain the "why" behind each answer strategy
- Education paths show you what certs or degrees you actually need
- It's mobile-first and works on any device

We're also partnering with schools and universities to offer bulk access for career centers. If you're an educator, reach out — we'd love to work with your students.

Would love your feedback on what we can improve. What roles or features would you want to see?
