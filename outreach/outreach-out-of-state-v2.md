## Outreach Strategy

1. **Lead with Resume Tailor and Deep Dive** — these are the differentiators. No other free tool does this.
2. **Community colleges first** — more responsive, direct workforce development goals
3. **State college systems** — one yes unlocks multiple campuses (Delaware Tech = 4, CT State = 12, CUNY = 25)
4. **HBCUs and minority-serving institutions** — mission-aligned, often underserved
5. **LinkedIn warm intros** — connect with career center directors before emailing
6. **Follow up in 5 days** — no reply doesn't mean no interest
