About RoleCall
RoleCall is a free interview prep app that helps you practice for job interviews across 374+ career roles in 9 industries. Each role has 12 curated interview questions, expert tips, education paths, and certification guides.

Practice & Features
Choose a role, and RoleCall presents 12 role-specific interview questions one at a time. You answer out loud, then check your answer against expert tips and ideal response guidance. After completing all questions, you get a score and feedback.
Each question includes a professional tip explaining what interviewers are looking for and how to structure your answer. Pro users get these tips shown automatically; free users can reveal them manually.
Yes! Bookmark any question to revisit later. Free users get limited bookmarks; Pro users get unlimited.
Practice at least one mock interview per day to build your streak. Consistent practice is the best way to improve your interview skills.
