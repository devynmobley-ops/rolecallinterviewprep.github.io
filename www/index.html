Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect
RoleCall collects information you voluntarily provide when submitting role suggestions or general feedback, including the category, role name, reason, and your device platform (Android or web).

How We Use Your Information
Suggestions and feedback are used solely to improve the app by adding new roles and features. Data is stored securely and is not sold or shared with third parties.

Data Storage
Practice history, bookmarks, streaks, and preferences are stored locally on your device. Suggestions are transmitted to our secure Google Sheets for review.

Analytics
We track basic usage statistics (most practiced roles, session counts) locally on your device to improve the app experience. No data is sent to external analytics services.

Your Rights
You may clear all local data at any time by clearing the app's storage in your device settings. To request removal of submitted suggestions, contact us through the feedback feature.

Contact
For questions about this policy, use the General Feedback section in the app.
