Trust & Compliance

Built with student privacy at the core

We know that compliance is non-negotiable for schools. Here's how RoleCall handles student data — and why your IT department can say yes.

🔒

FERPA Compliant

RoleCall does not collect, store, or transmit education records. All practice data — scores, history, bookmarks — is stored locally on the student's device. No student PII is required to use the platform.

🛡

No PII Collected

Students can use RoleCall without providing their name, email, or student ID. Optional account creation (for cross-device sync) uses only an email address — no student ID, no grade data, no enrollment info.

💻

Data Stored on Device

Practice history, streaks, scores, and bookmarks are stored in the browser's local storage or on-device app storage. No server-side student data. Students can clear their data at any time.

🌐

HTTPS Everywhere

All traffic is encrypted via TLS. The site runs on Firebase Hosting with automatic SSL. No unencrypted data transmission. No third-party trackers beyond standard Google Analytics.

📜

Data Processing Agreement

We provide a signed DPA upon request for any institution that requires one. Our data handling practices are documented and auditable. View our full security overview.

👥

Admin Controls Available

Institutional partners can request aggregate usage dashboards showing how many students are active, which career paths are most popular, and engagement trends — all anonymized.

✅
No Risk to Try
RoleCall requires no student data to operate. There's nothing to sign, nothing to configure, and nothing to report. Students use it on their own devices, on their own time. If it doesn't work for your program, you've lost nothing.
