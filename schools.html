How It Works

Three simple steps to interview readiness

RoleCall is designed to be intuitive for students at any level, from freshmen exploring careers to seniors preparing for their first job.

Choose a Career Path

Students browse 374+ roles across 9 industries or search for a specific job title. Each role includes a full description, salary range, and required qualifications.

Practice with AI-Powered Mock Interviews

RoleCall generates realistic interview questions for each role. Students can practice answering out loud, review expert tips, and repeat as many times as they need.

Track Progress & Improve

Bookmark roles, maintain practice streaks, and see which areas need more work. Students build confidence with every session.
