Overview

Three Ways to Onboard Students

Choose the method that fits your institution's size and technical setup. All methods give students the same full access — the difference is how they sign up.

🎫
Promo Codes
Best for: any size institution

The simplest option. We generate a custom promo code for your institution. Students visit rollcallinterviewprep.com, create a free account, and enter the code to unlock Pro access.

How it works

  1. Email us your institution name and estimated student count
  2. We generate a unique promo code (e.g., HACC2026)
  3. You share the code via email, LMS, orientation, or flyers
  4. Students visit the site, sign up, and enter the code

Pros

  • No technical setup required
  • Works immediately — code ready within 24 hours
  • Students can use it on any device
  • Easy to distribute via any channel
✅ Easiest setup
📦
CSV Bulk Enrollment
Best for: 100+ students, department-wide rollouts

Upload a CSV of student emails and we'll pre-create accounts with Pro access. Students receive an email with their login credentials — no promo code entry needed.

How it works

  1. Prepare a CSV with student email addresses (one per line)
  2. Upload it using the tool below, or email it to us directly
  3. We create accounts and send welcome emails to each student
  4. Students click the link in their email and start practicing

CSV format

Your CSV file should have a single column with email addresses:

email
student1@school.edu
student2@school.edu
student3@school.edu

Pros

  • Students don't need to enter a promo code
  • Accounts are pre-created — lower friction
  • You can track which students have accounts
  • Works well for course-specific enrollment
⚠ Requires email list
🔒
Google Workspace SSO
Best for: Google Workspace schools (most colleges)

If your school uses Google Workspace (Gmail, Google Classroom, etc.), students can sign in with their school Google account. No separate RoleCall account needed — they use the same credentials they already have.

How it works

  1. We configure RoleCall to accept sign-ins from your school's Google Workspace domain
  2. Students visit rollcallinterviewprep.com and click "Sign in with Google"
  3. They authenticate with their school email (e.g., student@school.edu)
  4. Pro access is automatically granted based on their email domain

Pros

  • Zero friction — students use existing credentials
  • No promo codes to manage
  • Automatic Pro access for anyone with a school email
  • Familiar sign-in flow (Google SSO)

Requirements

  • Your school must use Google Workspace (not Microsoft 365 or other email providers)
  • We need your school's Google Workspace domain (e.g., school.edu)
  • Setup takes 1-2 business days
🛠 Requires domain config

LMS

LMS Integration

Want to add RoleCall directly to your Canvas, Blackboard, or Moodle course? Here are the options:

Option 1: Direct Link (Simplest)

Add a link to rollcallinterviewprep.com in your LMS course module or assignment. Students click the link, sign in, and practice. No LTI configuration needed.

Option 2: Embed via iframe

Embed RoleCall directly in your LMS page using an iframe. Students never leave the LMS. Add this to your course page:

<iframe src="https://rollcallinterviewprep.com" width="100%" height="600" style="border:none;border-radius:12px"></iframe>

FAQ

Common Questions

Can students use RoleCall without creating an account?

Yes. All core functionality (browsing roles, viewing questions, reading expert tips) works without an account. Accounts are only needed for progress tracking, bookmarks, streaks, and cross-device sync.

What if a student already has a RoleCall account?

If they used a personal email before, they can enter the promo code on their existing account. If we're doing CSV enrollment with their school email, we'll link it to their existing account if one exists.

Can we restrict access to only our students?

With Google Workspace SSO, yes — only students with your school's email domain can sign in. With promo codes, anyone with the code can use it, so keep it within your institution.

How do we revoke access after a semester ends?

Promo codes can be deactivated at any time. CSV-enrolled accounts can be deactivated in bulk. Google SSO access is revoked when we remove your domain from the allowed list. Just let us know when you want to end access.

Is there a limit to how many students we can enroll?

No hard limit. Free pilots support up to 200 students. For larger deployments, we'll set up institutional pricing. The platform handles thousands of concurrent users without issues.
