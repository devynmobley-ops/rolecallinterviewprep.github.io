Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Information We Collect

RoleCall collects information you voluntarily provide when submitting role suggestions or general feedback, including the category, role name, reason, and your device platform (Android or web). If you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication data through Firebase Authentication.

How We Use Your Information

Suggestions and feedback are used solely to improve the app by adding new roles and features. Account information is used to manage your subscription and sync your access across devices. Data is stored securely and is not sold or shared with third parties.

Data Storage

Practice history, bookmarks, streaks, and preferences are stored locally on your device. Suggestions are transmitted to our secure backend for review. Subscription data is managed through Stripe and stored in Firebase Firestore.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand general usage patterns (such as most practiced roles and session counts). This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users. You can opt out of Google Analytics by using browser extensions or adjusting your device settings.

Third-Party Services

RoleCall uses the following third-party services:

Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, and hosting

Stripe — payment processing for Pro subscriptions (we do not store your payment details)

Google Analytics — usage analytics

Your Rights

You may clear all local data at any time by clearing the app's storage in your device settings. To request deletion of your account or submitted suggestions, contact us through the feedback feature in the app or email us at the address below.

Children's Privacy

RoleCall is suitable for users of all ages, including students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided personal information without consent, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

For questions about this policy, email us at rollcallinterviewprep@outlook.com or use the General Feedback section in the app.
