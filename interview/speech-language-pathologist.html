Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you assess and diagnose speech and language disorders?
Tip: Discuss standardized testing, informal assessment, case history, observation, and how you differentiate between various communication disorders.
Q2
Describe your experience developing individualized treatment plans.
Tip: Show goal-setting based on assessment findings, patient/family priorities, evidence-based approaches, and measurable objectives with timelines.
Q3
How do you work with patients who have swallowing disorders?
Tip: Discuss clinical swallow evaluation, modified barium swallow, diet modification, compensatory strategies, and dysphagia exercises.
Q4
What is your experience with pediatric language development?
Tip: Discuss milestones, early intervention, play-based therapy, parent coaching, articulation vs phonological disorders, and school-based services.
