Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you navigate the government procurement process?
Tip: Show understanding of RFP/RFQ, SAM.gov, GSA schedules, and federal/state/local procurement rules.
Q2
What is your experience writing government proposals?
Tip: Discuss compliance matrix, technical volumes, past performance, and pricing strategies for government contracts.
Q3
How do you identify government opportunities?
Tip: Mention GovWin, SAM.gov, agency forecasts, incumbent analysis, and relationship-based intelligence.
Q4
What contract types have you worked with?
Tip: Show experience with firm-fixed-price, cost-plus, time-and-materials, and IDIQ contracts.
