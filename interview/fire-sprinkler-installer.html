Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you read and interpret fire sprinkler system blueprints?
Tip: Show understanding of shop drawings, hydraulic calculations, pipe sizing, head placement, and code requirements.
Q2
Describe your experience with different types of sprinkler systems.
Tip: Discuss wet, dry, pre-action, deluge, and foam systems, and when each is used.
Q3
How do you ensure code compliance during installation?
Tip: Show knowledge of NFPA 13, local amendments, inspection requirements, and working with fire marshals.
Q4
What pipe materials and joining methods do you use?
Tip: Discuss steel (threaded, grooved), CPVC, copper, and the advantages of each in different applications.
