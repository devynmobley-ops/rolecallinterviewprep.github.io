Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you assess cognitive decline in elderly patients?
Tip: Discuss MMSE, MoCA screening, behavioral observation, family input, and differentiating dementia from delirium.
Q2
Describe your experience managing polypharmacy in older adults.
Tip: Show understanding of drug interactions, Beers Criteria, medication reconciliation, and deprescribing conversations.
Q3
How do you prevent falls in a geriatric care setting?
Tip: Discuss risk assessments, environmental modifications, exercise programs, medication review, and hip protectors.
Q4
What is your approach to pain management in elderly patients?
Tip: Show age-appropriate assessment tools, multimodal approaches, avoiding over-sedation, and balancing pain relief with function.
