Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you assist clients with daily living activities?
Tip: Show knowledge of bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting, and feeding assistance while maintaining client dignity.
Q2
Describe your experience with medication reminders and management.
Tip: Discuss medication schedules, documentation, recognizing side effects, and knowing scope of practice (not administering).
Q3
How do you handle a client who refuses care?
Tip: Show patience, respect for autonomy, gentle persuasion, documenting refusals, and communicating with supervisors and family.
Q4
What is your approach to meal preparation for clients with dietary restrictions?
Tip: Discuss special diets (diabetic, low-sodium, pureed), food safety, and preparing appealing meals within guidelines.

Education & Certifications

Degrees

  • No degree required
  • High school diploma
  • CNA training program

Certifications

  • CNA (Certified Nursing Assistant)
  • HHA (Home Health Aide) Certification
  • CPR/First Aid

Career Paths

  • State-approved HHA training (75+ hours)
  • CNA program through community college or vocational school
  • On-the-job training + state competency exam
