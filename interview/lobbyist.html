Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you develop a legislative strategy for an issue?
Tip: Show issue analysis, stakeholder mapping, bill drafting, coalition building, and timeline management.
Q2
Describe your experience with state or federal legislative processes.
Tip: Show knowledge of committee processes, floor procedures, amendment strategies, and the role of staff.
Q3
How do you build relationships with legislators and their staff?
Tip: Show consistent engagement, providing credible information, understanding their priorities, and being a trusted resource.
Q4
What is your approach to coalition building?
Tip: Discuss identifying allies, shared messaging, coordinated advocacy, and managing diverse interests within a coalition.
