Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you drive sales performance in your store?
Tip: Discuss visual merchandising, sales goals, coaching staff, promotions, and using data to optimize product placement and inventory.
Q2
Describe your approach to staff management and development.
Tip: Cover hiring, training, scheduling, performance reviews, motivation, and creating a positive team culture.
Q3
How do you handle inventory management and shrinkage?
Tip: Discuss cycle counts, loss prevention, receiving procedures, and how you engage staff in shrinkage reduction.
Q4
Tell me about a time you dealt with a difficult customer situation.
Tip: Show de-escalation skills, finding solutions, empowering staff, and turning a negative into a positive experience.
