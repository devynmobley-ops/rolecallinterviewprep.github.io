Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you translate a script's world into a visual design?
Tip: Show your creative process: research, mood boards, concept art, collaborating with the director, and building a visual language.
Q2
Describe your experience with different production scales.
Tip: Show versatility: theater, film, television, events, or commercials, and how budget and timeline affect your approach.
Q3
How do you manage a design budget and allocate resources?
Tip: Show prioritization, material sourcing, reuse/rental strategies, and cost-effective solutions without compromising vision.
Q4
What is your experience with set construction and fabrication?
Tip: Discuss working with scenic shops, carpentry knowledge, material choices, and overseeing build quality.
