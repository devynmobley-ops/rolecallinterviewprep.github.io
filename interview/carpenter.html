Sample Interview Questions

Q1
What types of carpentry projects are you most experienced with?
Tip: Discuss framing, finish carpentry, cabinetry, commercial, or residential. Show breadth and depth, and any specialties you have developed.
Q2
How do you read and work from blueprints and building plans?
Tip: Show ability to interpret dimensions, specifications, and building codes from drawings. Discuss how you plan material lists from plans.
Q3
Describe a complex carpentry project you completed.
Tip: Use STAR. Show planning, measurement, problem-solving, and craftsmanship. Discuss challenges and how you overcame them.
Q4
How do you ensure accuracy in measurements and cuts?
Tip: Discuss measuring techniques, double-checking, using quality tools, accounting for material thickness, and the importance of precision in carpentry.
