Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you develop your creative vision for a film project?
Tip: Show how you interpret scripts, create visual references, develop a shooting style, and communicate your vision to the crew.
Q2
Describe your process for working with actors.
Tip: Discuss casting, rehearsal techniques, giving direction on set, building trust, and eliciting authentic performances.
Q3
How do you collaborate with your department heads (DP, production designer, editor)?
Tip: Show leadership style, clear communication of intent while allowing creative input, and maintaining a unified vision.
Q4
What is your approach to storyboarding and shot planning?
Tip: Discuss visual storytelling, shot lists, coverage strategy, planned vs spontaneous shooting, and efficiency on set.
