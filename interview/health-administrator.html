Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you ensure compliance with healthcare regulations?
Tip: Discuss specific regulations (HIPAA, CMS, Joint Commission), audit processes, staff training, and how you stay current with changes.
Q2
Describe your experience with healthcare revenue cycle management.
Tip: Cover billing, coding, claims processing, denial management, and how you improved collections or reduced denials.
Q3
How do you handle staff shortages and scheduling challenges?
Tip: Discuss contingency planning, cross-training, agency staffing, overtime management, and maintaining quality of care.
Q4
What EHR systems have you implemented or managed?
Tip: Discuss implementation challenges, training, workflow optimization, interoperability, and vendor management.
