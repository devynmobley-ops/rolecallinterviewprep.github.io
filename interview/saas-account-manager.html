Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you drive adoption and expansion within existing SaaS accounts?
Tip: Show health score monitoring, QBRs, identifying upsell triggers, and aligning product value with customer business goals.
Q2
Describe your approach to reducing churn in a SaaS business.
Tip: Discuss early warning signals, onboarding optimization, customer success playbooks, and proactive engagement strategies.
Q3
How do you conduct a quarterly business review (QBR)?
Tip: Show preparation, ROI documentation, roadmap sharing, executive alignment, and setting mutual goals for the next quarter.
Q4
What metrics do you track for account health?
Tip: Mention NRR (Net Revenue Retention), product usage data, NPS, support ticket trends, and feature adoption rates.
