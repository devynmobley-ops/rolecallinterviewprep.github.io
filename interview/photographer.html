Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you approach a photo shoot from concept to delivery?
Tip: Show a structured process: client consultation, mood boards, location scouting, shooting, culling, editing, and delivery. Emphasize preparation.
Q2
Describe your experience with different types of photography.
Tip: Discuss portrait, event, product, landscape, or commercial work. Show versatility and depth in your specialty areas.
Q3
How do you handle lighting in challenging conditions?
Tip: Discuss natural light, off-camera flash, reflectors, diffusers, and how you adapt to low light, harsh sun, or mixed lighting.
Q4
What camera equipment and editing software do you use?
Tip: Be specific about bodies, lenses, lighting gear, Lightroom, Photoshop. Focus on how you choose equipment for different situations.
