Sample Interview Questions

Q1
Walk me through your design process from brief to final deliverable.
Tip: Show a structured process: research, mood boards, concepts, iterations, feedback, and delivery. Emphasize thinking over software skills.
Q2
How do you handle feedback and criticism of your designs?
Tip: Show maturity: separating personal feelings from work, asking clarifying questions, and using feedback to improve the work.
Q3
Describe a project where you had to balance creative vision with client requirements.
Tip: Show flexibility, communication skills, and ability to find solutions that satisfy both aesthetic standards and business goals.
Q4
How do you stay inspired and current with design trends?
Tip: Mention specific sources: Dribbble, Behance, design books, art, nature. Show you distinguish between trends and timeless principles.
