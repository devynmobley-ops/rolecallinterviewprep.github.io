Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you develop a school-wide STEM curriculum aligned to standards?
Tip: Show integration of NGSS, math standards, cross-disciplinary connections, and progressive skill building across grade levels.
Q2
Describe your approach to hands-on, project-based STEM learning.
Tip: Show design thinking, engineering design process, maker spaces, and real-world problem solving in the classroom.
Q3
How do you support teachers in integrating STEM across subjects?
Tip: Discuss co-planning, professional development, resource curation, modeling lessons, and building teacher confidence.
Q4
What is your experience with STEM competitions and extracurriculars?
Tip: Mention robotics (FIRST, VEX), science olympiad, coding clubs, and how you organize and fund these programs.
