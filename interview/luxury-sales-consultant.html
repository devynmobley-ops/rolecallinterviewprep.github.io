Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you build relationships with high-net-worth clients?
Tip: Show discretion, personalized service, product expertise, remembering preferences, and creating a white-glove experience.
Q2
Describe your approach to understanding a client's style and needs.
Tip: Show active listening, lifestyle assessment, product recommendations tailored to the individual, and follow-up.
Q3
How do you handle a client who is undecided or comparing options?
Tip: Show expertise-based guidance, creating urgency without pressure, exclusivity, and building confidence in the purchase.
Q4
What is your experience with luxury brand standards and clienteling?
Tip: Discuss brand training, CRM for client history, VIP events, personalized outreach, and maintaining brand prestige.
