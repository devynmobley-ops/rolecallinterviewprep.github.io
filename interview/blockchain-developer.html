Sample Interview Questions

Q1
What blockchain platforms have you worked with and why did you choose them?
Tip: Compare Ethereum, Solana, Polygon, Hyperledger — show understanding of trade-offs in speed, cost, decentralization, and use case fit.
Q2
How do you ensure smart contract security?
Tip: Discuss common vulnerabilities (reentrancy, overflow), auditing tools (Slither, Mythril), formal verification, and following best practices like checks-effects-interactions.
Q3
Describe a dApp you built from concept to deployment.
Tip: Use STAR. Cover the problem, architecture decisions, smart contract design, frontend integration, testing, and deployment.
Q4
How do you handle gas optimization in smart contracts?
Tip: Discuss storage vs. memory usage, loop optimization, batching transactions, and choosing efficient data structures.
