Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you design a market research study from scratch?
Tip: Show ability to define objectives, choose methodology (qual/quant), design instruments, determine sample size, and plan analysis.
Q2
Describe your experience with different research methodologies.
Tip: Discuss surveys, focus groups, interviews, observational research, conjoint analysis, and when each is appropriate.
Q3
How do you ensure data quality in your research?
Tip: Discuss sampling methods, questionnaire design, bias mitigation, data cleaning, and validation techniques.
Q4
How do you translate research findings into actionable recommendations?
Tip: Show ability to go beyond data reporting, identify implications, and connect insights to business strategy.
