Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you optimize inventory levels across multiple locations?
Tip: Discuss demand forecasting, safety stock calculations, ABC analysis, centralization vs distribution, and inventory turnover metrics.
Q2
Describe your experience with procurement and vendor management.
Tip: Show skills in vendor selection, contract negotiation, performance monitoring, relationship management, and cost reduction strategies.
Q3
How do you handle supply chain disruptions?
Tip: Discuss risk assessment, contingency planning, alternative sourcing, communication with stakeholders, and building resilience.
Q4
What is your approach to demand forecasting?
Tip: Discuss statistical methods, market intelligence, collaboration with sales and marketing, seasonality, and forecast accuracy improvement.
