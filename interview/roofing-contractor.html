Sample Interview Questions

Q1
Describe your experience with different roofing materials and systems.
Tip: Show range: asphalt shingles, metal, tile, flat roofing (TPO, EPDM, built-up), and knowledge of when to recommend each.
Q2
How do you conduct a roof inspection and assessment?
Tip: Discuss visual inspection, moisture detection, identifying damage, documenting findings, and providing clear recommendations.
Q3
What is your approach to estimating and bidding roofing projects?
Tip: Show measurement techniques, material calculations, labor planning, overhead, profit margins, and presenting professional proposals.
Q4
How do you ensure safety on roofing job sites?
Tip: Discuss fall protection, ladder safety, weather monitoring, crew training, OSHA compliance, and creating a safety-first culture.
