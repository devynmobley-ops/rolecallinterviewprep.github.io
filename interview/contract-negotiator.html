Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you prepare for a contract negotiation?
Tip: Show research on the other party, understanding your leverage, identifying key terms, BATNA analysis, and setting objectives.
Q2
Describe your experience negotiating different types of contracts.
Tip: Show versatility: procurement, licensing, employment, partnership, or government contracts, and how approach differs.
Q3
How do you handle impasse or deadlock in negotiations?
Tip: Show creative problem-solving, reframing issues, bringing in decision-makers, taking breaks, and finding common ground.
Q4
What is your approach to risk allocation in contract terms?
Tip: Discuss indemnification, limitation of liability, warranties, insurance requirements, and balancing risk between parties.
