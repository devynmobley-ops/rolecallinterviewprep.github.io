Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you manage court filings and case docketing?
Tip: Show knowledge of filing systems, docket management, e-filing, and ensuring accuracy in court records.
Q2
Describe your experience with courtroom proceedings.
Tip: Show knowledge of court procedures, jury management, evidence handling, and supporting the judge during proceedings.
Q3
How do you handle public records requests?
Tip: Show knowledge of FOIA, state public records laws, redaction requirements, and balancing access with privacy.
Q4
What is your experience with case management systems?
Tip: Mention specific systems (Tyler Odyssey, Journal Technologies), data entry accuracy, and reporting capabilities.
