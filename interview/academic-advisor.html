Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you help students who are undecided about their major or career path?
Tip: Discuss assessment tools, career exploration, experiential learning, and a supportive non-directive approach to helping students find their path.
Q2
Describe how you manage a large caseload of students.
Tip: Show organization, prioritization, technology use, proactive outreach, and how you ensure no student falls through the cracks.
Q3
How do you handle a student in academic difficulty?
Tip: Discuss early intervention, identifying root causes, connecting to resources, building an action plan, and ongoing follow-up.
Q4
What is your approach to degree planning and course selection?
Tip: Show knowledge of curriculum requirements, prerequisite chains, graduation audits, and how you help students make informed decisions.
