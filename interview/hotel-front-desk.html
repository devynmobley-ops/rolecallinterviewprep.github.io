Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you handle a guest who is unhappy with their room?
Tip: Show empathy, options (upgrade, move, comp), speed of resolution, and how you turn a negative into a positive experience.
Q2
Describe your experience with property management systems.
Tip: Be specific: Opera, Fosse, Lightspeed. Discuss reservation management, check-in/out, billing, and how you use the system to personalize service.
Q3
How do you handle overbooking situations?
Tip: Show diplomacy, transparency, walking guests to other hotels with arrangements, compensation, and how you prevent future occurrences.
Q4
What is your approach to upselling rooms and amenities?
Tip: Discuss timing, reading the guest, offering value, and how you increase revenue without being pushy.
