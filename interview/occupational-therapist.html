Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you assess a new patient's functional limitations?
Tip: Discuss standardized assessments (FIM, COPM), observation, patient interview, chart review, and how you set measurable, meaningful goals.
Q2
Describe your experience developing treatment plans.
Tip: Show goal-setting using SMART criteria, activity analysis, grading and adapting tasks, patient-centered priorities, and outcome measurement.
Q3
How do you help patients regain independence in daily activities?
Tip: Discuss task training, adaptive equipment, environmental modification, energy conservation, and building confidence through graded success.
Q4
What is your experience with cognitive rehabilitation?
Tip: Discuss compensatory strategies, memory aids, cognitive exercises, metacognitive training, and how you help patients return to work or community roles.
