Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you build and maintain a financial model?
Tip: Discuss structure, assumptions, sensitivity analysis, version control, and how you ensure accuracy through auditing and peer review.
Q2
Describe a time your financial analysis influenced a major business decision.
Tip: Use STAR. Show the analysis, how you presented it, the decision that was made, and the measurable outcome.
Q3
How do you approach variance analysis and forecasting?
Tip: Discuss comparing actuals to budget, identifying root causes, communicating findings, and updating forecasts based on new data.
Q4
What financial tools and software are you proficient in?
Tip: Be specific: Excel (advanced formulas, pivot tables, VBA), SQL, Tableau, Bloomberg, and any ERP or planning tools you have used.
