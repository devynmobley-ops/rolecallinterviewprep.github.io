Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you assess a new patient's nutritional status?
Tip: Discuss anthropometric measurements, dietary recalls, lab values, medical history review, and identifying risk factors.
Q2
Describe your experience developing individualized meal plans.
Tip: Show ability to consider medical conditions, cultural preferences, budget, lifestyle, and evidence-based nutrition guidelines.
Q3
How do you handle patients who are resistant to dietary changes?
Tip: Show motivational interviewing, small achievable goals, education without judgment, and building rapport over time.
Q4
What is your approach to managing patients with diabetes or heart disease?
Tip: Discuss disease-specific guidelines (ADA, AHA), carb counting, sodium restriction, and coordinating with the care team.
