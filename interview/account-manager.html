Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you grow revenue within existing accounts?
Tip: Discuss upselling, cross-selling, expanding to new departments, and providing value that naturally leads to expansion.
Q2
Describe how you handle a dissatisfied key account.
Tip: Show urgency, root cause analysis, executive engagement, recovery plan, and follow-through. Use STAR with a real example.
Q3
How do you prioritize which accounts to focus on?
Tip: Discuss segmentation: revenue potential, strategic value, risk of churn, and how you allocate time accordingly.
Q4
What is your approach to building long-term client relationships?
Tip: Go beyond transactions. Discuss understanding their business, proactive communication, and becoming a trusted advisor.
