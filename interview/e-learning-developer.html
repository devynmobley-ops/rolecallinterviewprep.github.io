Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you design an engaging e-learning course from scratch?
Tip: Show ADDIE/SAM process, learning objectives alignment, storyboarding, interaction design, and user testing.
Q2
What authoring tools do you use and why?
Tip: Discuss Articulate Storyline, Rise, Captivate, H5P, and when to use each based on project requirements.
Q3
How do you ensure courses are accessible to all learners?
Tip: Show knowledge of WCAG, screen reader compatibility, captioning, keyboard navigation, and universal design principles.
Q4
Describe a time you improved a poorly performing e-learning module.
Tip: Use STAR. Show analysis of issues, redesign approach, and measurable improvement in completion or assessment scores.
