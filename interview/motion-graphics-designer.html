Sample Interview Questions

Q1
What is your creative process from brief to final delivery?
Tip: Show a structured approach: understanding the brief, mood boards, storyboarding, animation, client feedback rounds, and final delivery.
Q2
Describe your experience with different animation styles.
Tip: Show range: 2D, 3D, kinetic typography, character animation, explainer videos, UI animation, and which styles you are strongest in.
Q3
How do you handle client revisions and feedback?
Tip: Discuss managing expectations, revision limits, presenting options, explaining creative decisions, and maintaining quality through multiple rounds.
Q4
What software tools are you most proficient in?
Tip: Discuss After Effects, Cinema 4D, Blender, Premiere Pro, and any plugins or scripts you use to streamline your workflow.
