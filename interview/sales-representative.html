Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you approach prospecting and generating new leads?
Tip: Discuss specific methods: cold calling, email, LinkedIn, referrals, events, inbound. Give conversion rates if possible.
Q2
Describe your sales process from initial contact to close.
Tip: Show a structured approach: discovery, qualification, presentation, objection handling, closing, and follow-up. Use a framework like SPIN or MEDDIC.
Q3
How do you handle rejection?
Tip: Show resilience, learning from each rejection, not taking it personally, and how rejection fuels improvement in your approach.
Q4
Tell me about your biggest sale. What did you do differently?
Tip: Use STAR. Show strategy, persistence, relationship building, and how you navigated complexity. Quantify the deal.
