Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you design a data pipeline from source to destination?
Tip: Discuss ingestion, transformation, loading, orchestration (Airflow, Dagster), idempotency, and error handling at each stage.
Q2
Describe your experience with ETL vs ELT approaches.
Tip: Show understanding of when to transform (before or after loading), cloud warehouse capabilities, and performance trade-offs.
Q3
How do you ensure data quality across pipelines?
Tip: Mention validation checks, schema enforcement, data contracts, anomaly detection, and alerting on pipeline failures.
Q4
What is your experience with streaming data processing?
Tip: Discuss Kafka, Spark Streaming, Flink, or Kinesis. Show understanding of windowing, exactly-once semantics, and backpressure.
