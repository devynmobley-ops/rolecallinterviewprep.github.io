Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you read and interpret HVAC sheet metal shop drawings?
Tip: Show understanding of duct layouts, fittings, gauge specifications, insulation details, and coordinate with mechanical plans.
Q2
Describe your experience fabricating ductwork.
Tip: Discuss rectangular, round, and spiral duct fabrication, using plasma tables, shears, brakes, and roll formers.
Q3
How do you calculate duct sizes for proper airflow?
Tip: Show knowledge of CFM requirements, friction loss, velocity, duct calculators, and Manual D methodology.
Q4
What joining methods do you use for ductwork?
Tip: Discuss S-slips, drive cleats, flanges, S&D connections, sealant application, and SMACNA standards.
