Sample Interview Questions

Q1
Describe your experience with different flooring materials.
Tip: Show knowledge of hardwood, LVP/LVT, tile, carpet, laminate, and epoxy. Discuss proper installation for each.
Q2
How do you prepare a subfloor for flooring installation?
Tip: Show knowledge of leveling, moisture testing, cleaning, underlayment, and addressing subfloor issues.
Q3
What is your experience with hardwood floor installation?
Tip: Discuss nail-down, glue-down, floating methods, acclimation, expansion gaps, and finishing techniques.
Q4
How do you handle tile layout and installation?
Tip: Show knowledge of substrate prep, thin-set application, tile cutting, grouting, and achieving consistent spacing.
