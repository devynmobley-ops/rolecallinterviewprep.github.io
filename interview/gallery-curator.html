Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you develop a curatorial vision for an exhibition?
Tip: Show thematic thinking, research depth, narrative arc, and how you select works that create a cohesive experience.
Q2
Describe your approach to acquiring and selecting artworks.
Tip: Discuss artist relationships, gallery partnerships, provenance research, condition assessment, and acquisition committee processes.
Q3
How do you write exhibition catalog essays and wall text?
Tip: Show academic writing skills, accessibility for general audiences, art historical context, and synthesizing complex ideas clearly.
Q4
What is your experience with exhibition design and installation?
Tip: Discuss spacing, lighting, sightlines, flow, pedestals/framing, and creating an immersive visitor experience.
