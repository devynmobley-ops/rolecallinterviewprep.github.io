Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you design and conduct political research?
Tip: Discuss hypothesis formation, data collection methods, statistical analysis, and peer review process.
Q2
Describe your expertise in a specific area of political science.
Tip: Show depth in comparative politics, international relations, political theory, public policy, or American politics.
Q3
How do you analyze election data and polling?
Tip: Discuss polling methodology, sampling, margin of error, aggregation, and interpreting results critically.
Q4
What is your experience with policy analysis and recommendations?
Tip: Show ability to research policy options, assess trade-offs, model outcomes, and present evidence-based recommendations.
