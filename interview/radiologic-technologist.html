Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you ensure patient safety during imaging procedures?
Tip: Discuss ALARA principle, shielding, proper positioning, pregnancy screening, and contrast allergy protocols.
Q2
Describe your experience with different imaging modalities.
Tip: Show proficiency across X-ray, CT, MRI, fluoroscopy, and any specialized modalities. Discuss when each is clinically appropriate.
Q3
How do you handle a patient who is claustrophobic or anxious about an imaging procedure?
Tip: Show empathy, communication techniques, offering breaks, explaining the process, and involving the care team when needed.
Q4
What is your approach to image quality assurance?
Tip: Discuss daily QC checks, phantom imaging, artifact recognition, equipment calibration, and when to reject and repeat images.
