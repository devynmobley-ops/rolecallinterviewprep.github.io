Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you assess the needs of schools or districts you work with?
Tip: Discuss data analysis, stakeholder interviews, classroom observations, needs assessment frameworks, and identifying root causes.
Q2
Describe your experience with educational program evaluation.
Tip: Show evaluation methodology, data collection, analysis, reporting, and translating findings into actionable recommendations.
Q3
How do you stay current with education research and best practices?
Tip: Mention journals, conferences, professional networks, pilot programs, and how you synthesize research into practical guidance.
Q4
What is your approach to working with resistant or skeptical educators?
Tip: Show empathy, building trust, demonstrating value through quick wins, involving teachers in solutions, and respecting their expertise.
