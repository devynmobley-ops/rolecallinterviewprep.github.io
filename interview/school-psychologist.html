Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you conduct a psychoeducational evaluation?
Tip: Show systematic approach: referral review, assessment planning, testing administration, interpretation, and report writing.
Q2
Describe your experience with Response to Intervention (RTI/MTSS).
Tip: Discuss tiered support, progress monitoring, data-based decision making, and collaboration with teachers and administrators.
Q3
How do you communicate assessment results to parents?
Tip: Show ability to translate technical findings into understandable language, address concerns, and involve parents in planning.
Q4
What is your approach to crisis intervention in schools?
Tip: Discuss threat assessment, safety planning, grief counseling, post-crisis support, and working with emergency services.
