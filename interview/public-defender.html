Sample Interview Questions

Q1
Why are you passionate about public defense?
Tip: Show commitment to constitutional rights, equal justice, and understanding the Sixth Amendment.
Q2
How do you manage a heavy caseload?
Tip: Discuss prioritization, case triage, plea negotiation strategy, and time management systems.
Q3
What is your experience with criminal trial work?
Tip: Show jury trial experience, opening/closing statements, cross-examination, and witness preparation.
Q4
How do you build trust with skeptical clients?
Tip: Show active listening, transparency about process, consistent communication, and genuine advocacy.
