Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you plan and manage entertainment programming for a cruise?
Tip: Show ability to create a diverse schedule, manage multiple venues, balance demographics, and maintain energy throughout the voyage.
Q2
Describe your experience hosting live events and shows.
Tip: Show MC skills, crowd engagement, improvisation, and ability to connect with audiences of all backgrounds.
Q3
How do you manage a team of entertainment staff and performers?
Tip: Show leadership, scheduling, conflict resolution, rehearsal management, and maintaining morale at sea.
Q4
What is your approach to creating a guest experience itinerary?
Tip: Discuss daily programming, port day vs sea day activities, themed nights, and variety across the voyage.
