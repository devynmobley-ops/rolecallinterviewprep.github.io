Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you develop a visual style for a project or client?
Tip: Discuss mood boards, reference gathering, style exploration, client alignment, and balancing personal style with project needs.
Q2
Describe your process from concept sketch to final illustration.
Tip: Show workflow: thumbnail sketches, rough drafts, client feedback rounds, refinement, and final delivery in appropriate formats.
Q3
What mediums and tools do you work with?
Tip: Mention traditional (ink, watercolor, gouache) and digital (Procreate, Photoshop, Illustrator, Clip Studio). Show versatility.
Q4
How do you handle client feedback and revisions?
Tip: Show professionalism: clear revision policies, interpreting subjective feedback, offering alternatives, and managing expectations.
