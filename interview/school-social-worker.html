Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you conduct a psychosocial assessment for a student?
Tip: Show a holistic approach: interviews with student and family, review of records, behavioral observation, and using validated screening tools.
Q2
Describe how you handle a student who discloses abuse or neglect.
Tip: Show mandatory reporting knowledge, documentation, sensitivity, and follow-up with CPS and school administration.
Q3
How do you support students experiencing homelessness or housing instability?
Tip: Discuss McKinney-Vento Act, connecting families with resources, school stability, and coordinating with community agencies.
Q4
What is your approach to crisis intervention in a school setting?
Tip: Show de-escalation skills, safety planning, coordination with counselors and administration, and post-crisis follow-up.
