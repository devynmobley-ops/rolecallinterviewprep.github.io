Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you build a targeted prospecting list?
Tip: Show ICP understanding, using tools (LinkedIn Sales Nav, ZoomInfo, Apollo), and qualifying leads before outreach.
Q2
What is your approach to cold outreach (email, phone, social)?
Tip: Discuss personalization, value-first messaging, multi-touch cadences, and A/B testing approaches for optimization.
Q3
How do you handle common objections during cold calls?
Tip: Show preparation for 'not interested,' 'already have a solution,' 'send me an email,' and techniques for reopening conversations.
Q4
Describe a time you exceeded your quota. What did you do differently?
Tip: Use STAR. Show specific tactics, consistency, time management, and how you stayed motivated.
