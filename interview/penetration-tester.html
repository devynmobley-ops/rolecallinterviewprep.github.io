Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you scope and plan a penetration test engagement?
Tip: Discuss rules of engagement, target identification, testing methodology (OWASP, PTES), and communication with stakeholders.
Q2
Describe your methodology for web application penetration testing.
Tip: Walk through recon, mapping, vulnerability identification, exploitation, and post-exploitation. Reference OWASP Top 10.
Q3
What tools do you use for network penetration testing?
Tip: Mention Nmap, Metasploit, Burp Suite, Cobalt Strike, BloodHound, and custom scripts. Show depth beyond tool names.
Q4
How do you approach privilege escalation on a compromised system?
Tip: Discuss kernel exploits, misconfigured services, credential harvesting, token impersonation, and lateral movement techniques.
