Sample Interview Questions

Q1
Walk me through your editing workflow from raw footage to final delivery.
Tip: Show a structured process: logging footage, assembly, rough cut, fine cut, color, audio mix, and export. Discuss how you organize projects.
Q2
What editing software are you most proficient in?
Tip: Be specific: Premiere Pro, Final Cut, DaVinci Resolve, Avid. Discuss what you have created and why you chose particular tools for projects.
Q3
How do you approach storytelling through editing?
Tip: Discuss pacing, rhythm, emotional arc, J and L cuts, match cuts, and how you make editorial choices that serve the narrative.
Q4
Describe a project where you worked with a difficult or demanding client.
Tip: Use STAR. Show communication, revision management, setting expectations, and how you delivered a result the client was happy with.
