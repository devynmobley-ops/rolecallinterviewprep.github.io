Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you ensure safety when working inside a boiler vessel?
Tip: Discuss confined space entry procedures, atmospheric testing, ventilation, lockout/tagout, buddy system, and rescue planning.
Q2
Describe your experience with boiler tube replacement and repair.
Tip: Show knowledge of tube rolling, welding, expanding, hydrostatic testing, and identifying tube failure causes (erosion, corrosion, overheating).
Q3
How do you read and interpret boiler blueprints and code requirements?
Tip: Discuss ASME code knowledge, blueprint symbols, weld maps, material specs, and using drawings to plan work sequences.
Q4
What welding processes are you proficient in for boiler work?
Tip: Discuss SMAW, GTAW, GMAW for boiler applications, different positions, code-compliant welds, and certifications held.
