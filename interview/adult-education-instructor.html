Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you assess the learning needs and goals of adult students?
Tip: Show intake assessment processes, goal-setting conversations, recognizing diverse backgrounds, and creating individualized learning plans.
Q2
Describe your approach to teaching adults who may have had negative prior educational experiences.
Tip: Show trauma-informed teaching, building confidence, celebrating progress, creating a safe learning environment, and patience.
Q3
How do you make content relevant to adult learners' lives and careers?
Tip: Show real-world application, connecting to workplace scenarios, using authentic materials, and honoring adult life experience as a resource.
Q4
What is your experience with teaching foundational literacy and numeracy?
Tip: Discuss scaffolding from basics, multisensory approaches, patience with varying skill levels, and connecting to daily life applications.
