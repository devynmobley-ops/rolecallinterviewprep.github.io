Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you build rapport with clients in crisis situations?
Tip: Discuss active listening, meeting clients where they are, trauma-informed approach, establishing safety, and building trust under pressure.
Q2
Describe your experience with case management and documentation.
Tip: Show thorough intake assessment, service planning, progress notes, resource coordination, and maintaining organized case files.
Q3
How do you handle mandated reporting and ethical dilemmas?
Tip: Discuss knowledge of reporting laws, ethical frameworks, consultation with supervisors, and balancing client autonomy with duty to protect.
Q4
What is your experience working with diverse populations?
Tip: Show cultural humility, awareness of systemic barriers, anti-oppressive practice, and how you adapt services to meet diverse needs.
