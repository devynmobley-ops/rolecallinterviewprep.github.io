Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you evaluate a patient for an orthotic device?
Tip: Show assessment process: range of motion, strength, skin integrity, functional goals, and biomechanical analysis.
Q2
Describe your experience with different types of orthotic devices.
Tip: Discuss AFOs, KAFOs, TLSO, cervical orthotics, upper extremity devices, and condition-specific applications.
Q3
How do you take measurements and create custom orthoses?
Tip: Show knowledge of casting, CAD/CAM design, material selection, and fabrication techniques.
Q4
What is your approach to fitting and adjusting orthotic devices?
Tip: Discuss alignment, pressure distribution, patient comfort, gait analysis, and iterative adjustments.
