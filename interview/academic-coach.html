Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you establish coaching relationships with teachers?
Tip: Discuss trust-building, voluntary vs assigned coaching, confidentiality, and goal-setting partnerships.
Q2
Describe your approach to classroom observation and feedback.
Tip: Show non-evaluative observation, specific feedback, modeling, and collaborative problem-solving.
Q3
How do you help teachers implement new instructional strategies?
Tip: Discuss co-planning, co-teaching, resource sharing, and gradual release of responsibility.
Q4
What is your experience with data-driven coaching?
Tip: Show ability to analyze student data, identify instructional gaps, and guide teachers toward evidence-based practices.
