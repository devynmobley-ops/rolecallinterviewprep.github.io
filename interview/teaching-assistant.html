Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you support the lead teacher in managing the classroom?
Tip: Show proactive assistance, behavior management, individual student support, and anticipating teacher needs during instruction.
Q2
Describe how you work with small groups or individual students.
Tip: Show differentiated instruction, patience, scaffolding techniques, and adapting to different learning styles and paces.
Q3
How do you handle a student who is disruptive during class?
Tip: Show calm responses, de-escalation, positive reinforcement, and knowing when to involve the lead teacher.
Q4
What is your approach to helping students with special needs?
Tip: Show awareness of IEPs/504s, accommodations, assistive technology, and collaborating with special education staff.
