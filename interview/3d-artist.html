Sample Interview Questions

Q1
What is your 3D modeling workflow from concept to final render?
Tip: Discuss reference gathering, blocking, sculpting, retopology, UV unwrapping, texturing, lighting, and rendering pipeline.
Q2
Describe your experience with different 3D software.
Tip: Compare Blender, Maya, 3ds Max, ZBrush, Houdini. Discuss your primary tools and how you choose based on project needs.
Q3
How do you approach texturing and material creation?
Tip: Discuss PBR workflow, Substance Painter/Designer, procedural textures, hand-painting, and achieving realistic or stylized looks.
Q4
What is your experience with character modeling and rigging?
Tip: Show knowledge of anatomy, topology for animation, skeletal rigging, blend shapes, and preparing models for production.
