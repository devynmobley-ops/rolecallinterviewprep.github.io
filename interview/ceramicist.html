Sample Interview Questions

Q1
Describe your experience with different ceramic techniques.
Tip: Show knowledge of wheel throwing, handbuilding (slab, coil, pinch), slip casting, and surface decoration methods.
Q2
How do you develop a glaze palette and test new glazes?
Tip: Discuss glaze chemistry basics, line blends, test tiles, firing schedules, and achieving consistent results.
Q3
What is your experience with kiln operation and firing?
Tip: Show knowledge of bisque and glaze firing, oxidation vs reduction, temperature control, and kiln maintenance.
Q4
How do you approach designing a collection or body of work?
Tip: Show concept development, material exploration, iterative prototyping, and thematic cohesion.
