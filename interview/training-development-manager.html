Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you assess training needs across an organization?
Tip: Discuss needs analysis methods: surveys, interviews, performance data review, skills gap analysis, and business goal alignment.
Q2
Describe how you design a training program from scratch.
Tip: Show ADDIE or similar framework: analyze, design, develop, implement, evaluate. Include learning objectives and adult learning principles.
Q3
How do you measure training effectiveness?
Tip: Discuss Kirkpatrick's four levels: reaction, learning, behavior, results. Show you go beyond satisfaction surveys to business impact.
Q4
What is your experience with learning management systems?
Tip: Mention specific LMS platforms (Cornerstone, Docebo, SAP SuccessFactors), content management, reporting, and user experience.
