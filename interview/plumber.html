Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you diagnose a plumbing leak that is not immediately visible?
Tip: Show systematic approach: meter reading, pressure testing, inspection cameras, and logical elimination of possibilities.
Q2
Describe your experience with different piping materials.
Tip: Discuss copper, PEX, PVC, CPVC, cast iron. Show knowledge of when to use each and proper installation techniques.
Q3
How do you ensure code compliance in your plumbing work?
Tip: Discuss IPC/UPC familiarity, permit processes, inspection preparation, and how you stay current with code changes.
Q4
What is your approach to customer communication on a service call?
Tip: Show professionalism: explain the problem clearly, discuss options and costs, get approval before proceeding, and clean up after.
