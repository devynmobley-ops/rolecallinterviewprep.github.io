Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you establish and maintain cleaning standards across your team?
Tip: Discuss SOPs, checklists, training, inspections, quality audits, and how you ensure consistency across all areas.
Q2
Describe your approach to staff scheduling and workload distribution.
Tip: Show how you balance occupancy levels, staff availability, special requests, and fairness in task assignment.
Q3
How do you handle guest or resident complaints about cleanliness?
Tip: Show empathy, urgency, immediate response, root cause analysis, and follow-up to prevent recurrence.
Q4
What is your experience with inventory and supply management?
Tip: Discuss par levels, ordering, vendor relationships, cost control, and ensuring supplies are always available without overstocking.
