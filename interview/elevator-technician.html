Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you diagnose and repair common elevator malfunctions?
Tip: Show systematic troubleshooting: door issues, leveling problems, control system faults, and using wiring diagrams and service manuals.
Q2
Describe your experience with preventive maintenance on elevator systems.
Tip: Discuss inspection schedules, lubrication, wear part replacement, code compliance checks, and documentation.
Q3
How do you ensure compliance with elevator safety codes?
Tip: Show knowledge of ASME A17.1, local codes, inspection requirements, and how you stay current with code updates.
Q4
What types of elevator systems have you worked on?
Tip: Discuss hydraulic, traction, machine-room-less, freight elevators, and your experience level with each.

Education & Certifications

Degrees

  • No degree required
  • AS Electronics/Mechanical
  • Apprenticeship program

Certifications

  • CET (Certified Elevator Technician)
  • State Elevator Mechanic License
  • OSHA 10/30

Career Paths

  • NEIEP apprenticeship program (4 years)
  • Electronics training + on-the-job hours
  • NAEC CET exam + state licensing exam
