Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you conduct an initial patient assessment?
Tip: Discuss patient history, orthopedic and neurological exams, posture analysis, and diagnostic imaging interpretation.
Q2
Describe your approach to developing a treatment plan.
Tip: Show personalized care planning, frequency recommendations, measurable goals, and patient education.
Q3
What chiropractic techniques are you proficient in?
Tip: Mention diversified, Thompson, Activator, Cox flexion-distraction, and when you choose each technique.
Q4
How do you handle a patient not responding to treatment?
Tip: Show reassessment, technique modification, co-management with other providers, and honest communication.

Education & Certifications

Degrees

  • BS Biology/Pre-Chiro
  • BS Kinesiology

Certifications

  • State Chiropractic License
  • Board Certification (Diplomate)
  • CCE (Council on Chiropractic Education)

Career Paths

  • Accredited DC program (4 years) + clinical internship
  • National Board of Chiropractic Examiners exam + state license
  • Diplomate specialty certification after practice experience
