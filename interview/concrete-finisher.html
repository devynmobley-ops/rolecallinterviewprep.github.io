Sample Interview Questions

Q1
Describe your experience with different types of concrete finishing.
Tip: Show knowledge of trowel finish, broom finish, stamped concrete, exposed aggregate, polished concrete, and decorative techniques.
Q2
How do you ensure proper concrete mix quality before placement?
Tip: Discuss slump testing, air content testing, temperature checks, rejecting non-conforming loads, and coordination with the batch plant.
Q3
What is your approach to placing concrete in different weather conditions?
Tip: Show knowledge of hot weather (shading, fogging, retarders), cold weather (insulation, heated enclosures, accelerators), and wind protection.
Q4
How do you plan and execute a large concrete pour?
Tip: Discuss sequencing, crew coordination, equipment needs, cold joints, day joints, and managing logistics.
