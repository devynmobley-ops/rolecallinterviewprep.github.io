Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you generate leads and build your client pipeline?
Tip: Discuss networking, referrals, online marketing, open houses, community involvement, CRM use, and how you nurture relationships over time.
Q2
Describe your approach to pricing a property.
Tip: Show CMA skills, market analysis, understanding seller expectations, and how you balance competitive pricing with maximizing value.
Q3
How do you handle multiple offers on a property?
Tip: Discuss presenting all offers fairly, advising clients on terms vs price, escalation clauses, and managing expectations on both sides.
Q4
What is your negotiation strategy during a transaction?
Tip: Show preparation, understanding the other party's needs, creative problem-solving, maintaining professionalism, and knowing when to push or compromise.
