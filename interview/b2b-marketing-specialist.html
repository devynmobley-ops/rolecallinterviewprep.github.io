Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you develop a B2B marketing strategy for a new product?
Tip: Show ICP definition, messaging framework, channel selection, content plan, and demand generation tactics.
Q2
Describe your experience with account-based marketing (ABM).
Tip: Show account selection, personalized campaigns, sales alignment, multi-channel orchestration, and measurement.
Q3
How do you generate and nurture leads in B2B?
Tip: Discuss inbound (content, SEO, webinars), outbound (SDR, email), lead scoring, and nurture workflows.
Q4
What marketing automation platforms have you used?
Tip: Mention Marketo, HubSpot, Pardot, or ActiveCampaign. Show depth: segmentation, workflows, and attribution.
