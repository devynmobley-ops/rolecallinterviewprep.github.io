Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you handle an angry or frustrated customer?
Tip: Show empathy first (HEARD: hear, empathize, apologize, resolve, diagnose). Do not take it personally. Focus on solving the problem.
Q2
Describe a time you turned a negative customer experience into a positive one.
Tip: Use STAR. Show empathy, creativity, empowerment, and the outcome. Quantify if possible (retention, survey score).
Q3
How do you manage high call/chat volume while maintaining quality?
Tip: Discuss efficiency techniques, knowledge base usage, templates (without sounding robotic), and how you balance speed with thoroughness.
Q4
What CRM and customer service tools have you used?
Tip: Be specific: Zendesk, Salesforce, Freshdesk, Intercom. Discuss how you use them to track interactions and improve service.
