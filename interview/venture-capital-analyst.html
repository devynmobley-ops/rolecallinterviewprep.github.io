Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you evaluate an early-stage startup for investment?
Tip: Discuss team assessment, market sizing, product-market fit, traction metrics, unit economics, and competitive landscape.
Q2
Describe your approach to market sizing and analysis.
Tip: Show top-down and bottom-up approaches, TAM/SAM/SOM, data sources, and triangulation methods.
Q3
How do you assess a startup's founding team?
Tip: Show evaluation of domain expertise, complementary skills, execution history, coachability, and team dynamics.
Q4
What financial metrics matter most for early-stage companies?
Tip: Discuss MRR/ARR, burn rate, runway, CAC, LTV, gross margin, and how they vary by stage and business model.
