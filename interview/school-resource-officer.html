Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you build trust with students as a school resource officer?
Tip: Show approachability, consistent presence, mentorship, positive interactions, and being more than just enforcement.
Q2
Describe your approach to threat assessment in a school setting.
Tip: Discuss behavioral warning signs, reporting protocols, multidisciplinary teams, and intervention strategies.
Q3
How do you handle a student discipline situation that involves law enforcement?
Tip: Show understanding of when school discipline vs legal action is appropriate, de-escalation, and documentation.
Q4
What is your experience with emergency preparedness in schools?
Tip: Discuss active threat drills, reunification planning, communication systems, and coordination with first responders.
