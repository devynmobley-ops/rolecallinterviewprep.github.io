Sample Interview Questions

Q1
How do you assess a student's current level and learning needs?
Tip: Discuss diagnostic assessments, reviewing prior work, interviewing the student, identifying gaps, and setting baseline expectations.
Q2
Describe your approach to creating individualized learning plans.
Tip: Show how you set goals, choose materials, plan session structure, and adjust based on student progress and feedback.
Q3
How do you keep students engaged and motivated during tutoring sessions?
Tip: Discuss variety, student interests, positive reinforcement, achievable challenges, and building confidence through incremental success.
Q4
What strategies do you use when a student is struggling with a concept?
Tip: Show multiple approaches: visual aids, real-world examples, breaking down problems, scaffolding, and checking for misconceptions.
